: Basic Ludo Rules :

1.Objective: Move all 4 pieces from your starting area to the home.
2.Starting:
(a) Roll a 6 to move a piece out of the home area.
(b) Rolling a 6 gives an extra turn.
3.Movement:
(a) Pieces move clockwise based on dice rolls.
(b) Land on an opponentâ€™s piece to send it back to their home.
4.Safe Positions: Star-marked positions and starting starting are safe zones where pieces cannot be captured.
5.Entering Home: Pieces must complete a circuit and enter the home triangle with an exact roll.
6.Winning: First to get all 4 Pieces home wins.
